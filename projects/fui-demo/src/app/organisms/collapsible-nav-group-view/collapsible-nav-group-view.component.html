Collapsible Nav Group

The fui-collapsible-nav-group component provides a collapsible navigation group functionality, allowing users to toggle between displaying and hiding content within a navigation section. This component is designed to enhance user experience by optimizing space utilization and providing a clean interface for navigating through content.

The fui-collapsible-nav-group component consists of a header section and a body section. Users can toggle the visibility of the body section by clicking on the header. When collapsed, the body content is hidden, and when expanded, the content is revealed.
