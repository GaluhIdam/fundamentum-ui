Resizable container

This component is handy for various resizable containers. FuiResizableContainer uses the Angular Render Props technique to provide FuiResizablePanel and FuiResizableButton components for layout, and actions for custom handling collapse and resize functionality in your app. Wrap parts of your content with the FuiResizablePanel component and put the FuiResizableButton component between.
