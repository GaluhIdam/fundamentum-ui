Tooltip

FuiToast allows for small notes that appear in the bottom right of the screen. They should be used for ephemeral, live actions (think save complete or something just finished right now). They should not be used for historical actions (your report built 30 minutes ago). This means that a user should never be greeted with toasts when starting a session. Toasts should be brief and avoid long paragraphs of text or titling.
