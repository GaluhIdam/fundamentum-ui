Flyout

The flyout component in Angular is a sliding panel that appears from the side of the screen. It is typically used to display additional content, settings, or actions without navigating away from the current view. The flyout can include a header, body, and footer, providing a structured space for information, controls, and buttons like "Save" or "Cancel."
